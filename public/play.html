How To Play

The game is split into two modes, Edit Mode and Play Mode.

In Edit Mode the player can move objects around with the mouseto make a
path for the ball to take. In this mode the ball physics will be disabled.

In Play Mode the ball physics is enabled, and the ball will begin to move.
While in Play Mode you cannot move any of the objects. If the ball does not
successfully reach the end goal, it will be reset to its start position,
the game will renter Edit Mode and the you will have to adjust the position
of the objects before trying again.
