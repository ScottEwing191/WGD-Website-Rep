Game Description

In Doodle Course the goal is to get the ball to the end of the course but the course is not completed and you, the player must complete the level in order to reach the end goal.

Why play Doodle Course?

We have made Doodle Course user-friendly to people who haven't played much puzzle games before. We achieved this by creating a basic and straight forward problem-solving game with levels that begin easier and progresses in difficulty to give every player a chance to adapt to the games mechanics and gameplay without it being too frustrating.
