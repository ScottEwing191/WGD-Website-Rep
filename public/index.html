Game Description

In Doodle Course the goal is to get the ball to the end of the course
                    but the course is not completed and you the player must complete the
                    level in order to reach the end goal.

Why play Doodle Course?

We have made doodle jump user friendly to people who
                    havent played much puzzle games before. We achieved this
                    by creating a basic and straightforward problem solving
                    game with levels that begin easier and progresses in
                    difficulty to give every player a chance to adapt to the
                    games mechanics and gameplay without it being too frustrating.
