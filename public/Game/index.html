How To Play

The objective of the game is to get the ball to the end of the level. To do this you have to move platforms, jump pads and other objects around the level into positions where they can help the ball.

When the game starts the ball will be stationary, and you will be allowed to move the objects around. To get the ball to move, press the spacebar. If you would like the ball to return to its stationary position press the R key.

You will be unable to move the platforms or other objects around while the ball is moving.
